Since 2010

Publishing that puts the classroom first

Learning Resource Book Publications began as a small team of teachers and editors who believed textbooks could do more than transmit facts — they could shape how a child thinks. Over two decades later, that belief still anchors every title we print, from pre-primary rhyme books to senior-secondary science texts.

We work directly with subject experts and classroom teachers across India to build curriculum-aligned books that are rigorous without being rigid, and structured without being dull. Every manuscript passes through academic review, editorial refinement and classroom piloting before it reaches a single desk.

Teacher-built

Every title is co-developed with practising educators, not written in isolation.

Board-aligned

Content mapped to state and national curricula, updated each academic cycle.

Beyond the page

Digital companions and worksheets extend learning outside the textbook.

Today, more than 2,000 schools use Learning Resource Book titles in their classrooms. We remain, at heart, a company run by people who still think like teachers.
